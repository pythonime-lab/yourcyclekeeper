FAQ

Common questions

Everything you need to know about privacy, security, and how Your Cycle Keeper works.

Is my data really private?

Yes. Your Cycle Keeper has no server, no backend, and no analytics of any kind. Your data is encrypted with your PIN using AES-256-GCM via the browser's Web Crypto API and stored only in your browser's IndexedDB. We have no ability to access it even if we wanted to.

What happens if I forget my PIN?

For your security, there is no PIN recovery mechanism. If you forget your PIN, the encrypted data cannot be recovered and will be erased. This is intentional — it means no one can access your data without your PIN. We strongly recommend exporting an encrypted backup regularly.

Can I use this on my phone?

Yes. Your Cycle Keeper works in any modern mobile browser. You can also install it as a Progressive Web App (PWA) from your browser's "Add to Home Screen" option for a native app experience that works offline.

How accurate are the predictions?

Predictions are based on the Calendar Rhythm Method using your logged cycle history. They become more accurate as you log more cycles. This is an estimation tool only — not a contraceptive. Stress, illness, and medications can all shift cycle timing.

What if I clear my browser data?

Clearing browser cache is safe. However, clearing "cookies and site data" in your browser settings will erase your cycle data. Always export an encrypted backup before doing so. You can restore it anytime from the Settings screen.

Is this really free forever?

Yes. Your Cycle Keeper is open-source software under the MIT License. There is no premium tier, no subscription, and no ads — ever. Optional donations support development but are never required.
